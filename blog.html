Mastering Lowcountry Home Maintenance: The Ultimate Guide to Protecting Your Investment

Lowcountry homeowners face unique maintenance challenges from high humidity, salt air, and extreme weather. This comprehensive guide reveals essential strategies for protecting your investment through seasonal maintenance routines covering spring cleaning to winter preparations. Learn about humidity control, water damage prevention, exterior protection, HVAC maintenance, and professional inspections. Discover the importance of proper drainage systems, regular roof inspections, and plumbing checks to prevent costly damage. Create a detailed maintenance plan with documentation and budget 1-2% of your home's value annually to ensure long-term protection and preserve your property's structure, systems, and value.
